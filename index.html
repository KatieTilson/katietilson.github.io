Connecting people and building community

I am learning to become a full-stack technical marketer and a successful startup employee at Startup Institute, an 8-week career accelerator in Manhattan born out of TechStars.

No matter what I do or where life takes me, my Midwestern values are always a source of inspiration. When the Haiti earthquake hit, I took the idea of being a global citizen into action. I left the world of Coca-Cola advertising to connect people to build a more just world.

Bringing fifteen 30yr olds to Ghana with a human rights nonprofit, I saw how experiential education can build a community to make change. Creating a young professional program, I learned how to connect a community online and off to understand motivational patterns and the data behind what makes people engage.

As I battled the traditional pace of a nonprofit, I grew inspired by the motivated startup ecosystem that constantly iterates to improve and defy the status quo. Startup Institute was my chance to take a step back to figure out what I want, hone my marketing skills and meet a lot of people.

My goal is to bridge my passion for building relationships, storytelling, strategy and my positive attitude with a passion for strong brands and innovative businesses.
