Connecting people and building community

I am learning to become a full-stack technical marketer and a successful startup employee at Startup Institute, an 8-week career accelerator in Manhattan born out of TechStars.

No matter what I do or where life takes me, my Midwestern values are always a source of inspiration. I began my career in advertising working for one of the top two global beverage giants (you can guess which one!). The week of the Haiti earthquake I went to a fundraiser and saw the light, so to say. In the one-hour I spent learning about the devastations of the earthquake, I knew I wanted to spend my time making the world more just.

Three and a half years later, I am ready for another adventure. What I loves most about nonprofits is the passion it takes to make a difference. This is precisely what drew me to the startup world. I am inspired by the motivated and dedicated people that collaborate to go off the grid and defy the status quo.

My goal is to bridge my storytelling, analytical background and positive attitude with a passion for strong brands and innovative businesses.
