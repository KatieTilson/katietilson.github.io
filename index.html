Marketer and a hustler with passion to make the world a better place

Katie Tilson is learning to become a full-stack technical marketer at Startup Institute, an 8-week career accelerator in Manhattan born out of TechStars.

No matter what she does or where life takes her, Katie's Midwestern values will always be my source of inspiration. Katie began her career in advertising for one of those top two global beverage giants (you can guess which one!). The week of the Haiti earthquake she went to a fundraiser and realized she was in the wrong industry. In the one-hour she spent learning about the effects of the earthquake and how her dollars could make a difference, Katie knew she wanted to spend her time making the world more just.

Three and a half years later, Katie is ready for another adventure. What she loves most about nonprofits is the passion it takes to make a difference and this is precisely what has drawn her to the startup world. Katie is inspired by the motivated and dedicated people that collaborate to go off the grid and defy the status quo.

Katie Tilson's goal is to bridge my storytelling and analytical background with a passion for strong brands and innovative businesses.
